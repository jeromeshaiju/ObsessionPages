# About Me & My Projects

## Who I Am
I am an independent creator, developer, and blogger focused on building modern digital spaces and exploring new technologies. 

## What I Do
I design, build, and curate content for digital platforms. Currently, my primary focus is developing and branding **ObsessionPages**, a specialized blog and digital space.

## Projects & Focus Areas

### 🖥️ ObsessionPages

*ObsessionPages* is a tech-focused blog and content platform designed with a clean, engaging, and unique visual identity. 
* **The Brand Identity:** A distinct purple and cream color palette featuring a professional yet playful, comic-inspired cartoon aesthetic with bold block typography and signature yellow star accents.
* **The Vision:** Delivering tech insights, deep dives, and highly curated digital content in a compact, accessible format.

### 🛠️ Core Skills & Activities
* **Brand Strategy & Logo Design:** Iterative visual development, UI/UX aesthetics, and concept refinement.
* **Content Creation:** Writing and organizing articles, technical guides, and curated takes for web publishing.
* **Web Development:** Planning and executing structured digital platforms using markdown, web tools, and modern frameworks.
